02 The Problem
How many seller-facing official WeChat accounts does Amazon have? → 8 (AGS×3, Ads×2, AGL×2, AWS×1)

Have you — honestly — read all of them?

Maybe nobody does. Amazon DID publish the update, the deadline, the new tool, and the best practices — piece by piece, across 8 accounts. The answers are out there. But nobody reads all 8. And by the time they find it, it's too late.

This is NOT a content problem. It's a DELIVERY problem.
The right information exists — it just doesn't reach the right person at the right time.
